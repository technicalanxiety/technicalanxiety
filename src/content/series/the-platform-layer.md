## Overview

The platform layer is where infrastructure becomes a product. It's the abstraction that lets application teams focus on business logic while maintaining operational excellence.

## What You'll Learn

### Platform Thinking
- What makes something a platform vs. shared infrastructure
- Defining platform boundaries and interfaces
- Building platforms that teams actually want to use
- Measuring platform success

### Implementation Patterns
- Self-service capabilities without chaos
- Guardrails that enable rather than restrict
- Documentation and developer experience
- Platform evolution and versioning

### Organizational Dynamics
- Platform teams vs. infrastructure teams
- Building platform adoption
- Balancing flexibility and standardization
- Platform governance models

## Who This Is For

- **Platform Engineers** building internal platforms
- **Architects** designing platform strategies
- **Technical Leaders** establishing platform teams
- **Infrastructure Teams** evolving to platform thinking
