## Overview

Operations teams face constant pressure to adopt new technologies while maintaining existing systems. This series addresses how to evolve operations without breaking what works.

## What You'll Learn

### Change Management
- Balancing innovation and stability
- Introducing new tools and practices
- Managing operational risk during transitions
- Building team buy-in for change

### Operational Evolution
- Modernizing legacy operations
- Adopting DevOps practices
- Automation without losing control
- Measuring operational maturity

## Who This Is For

- **Operations Leaders** managing change
- **SREs** modernizing operations
- **Platform Teams** evolving practices
- **Anyone** navigating operational transformation

---

*Operations change is about evolution, not revolution. Stability and progress aren't opposites.*
