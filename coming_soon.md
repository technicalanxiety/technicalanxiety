## Content Guidelines

### Technical Depth
- Include working code examples with explanations
- Real-world scenarios from customer environments
- Honest assessments of limitations and tradeoffs
- Practical implementation guidance

### Structure Template
1. **Problem Statement** - What real problem does this solve?
2. **Context** - Why does this matter now?
3. **Solution Walkthrough** - Step-by-step implementation
4. **Gotchas & Lessons** - What goes wrong and how to avoid it
5. **When Not to Use** - Honest limitations
6. **Next Steps** - What to do after reading

### Voice & Tone
- Maintain authentic, practitioner voice
- "Here's what actually works" over "here's what's possible"
- Include failures and lessons learned
- Balance technical depth with strategic context
- AI content: "learning in public" framing
